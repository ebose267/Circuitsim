IEEE University of Benin Student Branch

The official student chapter of the Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers at UNIBEN. CEO of PCB Mentor, a platform empowering African engineers to master industry-standard PCB design and professional Design for Manufacturing (DFM) workflows. Through high-impact initiatives like global bootcamps, his brand bridges the gap between theoretical electronics and the practical ability to route and build physical, manufacturable hardware products.
